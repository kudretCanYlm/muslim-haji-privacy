Privacy Policy – Muslim Haji

Last Updated: 2.11.2025

Muslim Haji (“we,” “our,” or “the app”) is a mobile application designed to help users follow and improve their spiritual practices in an engaging and motivating way. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

The app may collect the following information:

  • a) Personal Information
    • When creating an account: email address, username.
    • Language preference and in-app settings.
  • b) Usage Information
    • Daily tasks, prayer times, dhikr (remembrance) tracking, and Hadith interactions.
    • Activity and progress in streaks, badges, and achievements.
    • Interactions with the Hacı Dede character and Qur’an learning activities.
  • c) Location Information
    • GPS data for finding nearby mosques (only with your permission).
  • d) Device and Technical Information
    • Device type, operating system, app version, and crash reports.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Managing streaks, badges, and daily activity tracking.
  • Sending notifications for prayer times, Adhan alerts, dhikr reminders, and religious events.
  • Personalizing your experience with the Hacı Dede mascot.
  • Tracking Qur’an reading, listening, and exercises.
  • Improving app performance, features, and user experience.
  • Providing multilingual content and translations.
  • Conducting statistical analysis and understanding user behavior.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • Personal information is never sold or shared with third parties.
  • Content you choose to share (images, text, prayers) may be visible to other users according to your chosen settings.
  • Information may be shared with authorities if required by law or for security purposes.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The app may use cookies or similar technologies to improve your experience, remember preferences, and analyze usage.

5. Security

We prioritize the security of your information. Data is protected with appropriate technical and administrative measures to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. If we discover personal data from a child without parental consent, it will be deleted immediately.

7. Your Rights

  • You can access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • You can manage notifications and location permissions at any time.
  • You may delete your account at any time.

8. Motivational and User-Friendly Approach

We designed Muslim Haji not just as a tracking tool but as a motivating spiritual companion. With Hacı Dede and daily challenges, users can build and maintain healthy spiritual habits in an engaging and fun way.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be notified in-app, and significant changes will be highlighted for users.
